Obtain your boarding pass between 48hours and 1hour before your flight departure time. For flights to/from US 24hours before departure time.

EXAMPLES OF VALID LOCATORS:
69PFS7: A number followed by a combination of 4 or 5 letters or numbers.
Y9PFS7: Y followed by a combination 4 or 5 letters or numbers.
Z9PFS7: Z followed by a combination of 4 or 5 letters or numbers.
9960003469672: 996 followed by 10 digits with no hyphen.

* Check your booking: If your flight is operated by a partner airline you must chekc-in through their website.
* Remember that you can only obtain your boarding pass from 48 hours to 1 hour before flight departure time. If your flight is departing in more than 48h you can reserve your seat.
* You cannot check-in online if you have requested any special requirements.
